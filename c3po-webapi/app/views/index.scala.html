What is c3po?

c3po is a content profiling tool used during preservation planning. It uses the meta data extracted from digital objects and aggregates it in order to support the analysis during preservation planning activities. Furthermore, c3po provides representative samples from collections based on different algorithms.

It offers different data export facilities, so that planners can analyze the content even more and obtain deeper knowledge of the objects they are trying to preserve.
